August 26, 2019 - Vesicode announces the development of novel Extracellular Vesicle profiling method.

Vesicode today announced the development of a Novel method to profile surface proteins of individual exosomes. In this new study, published in Nature Communications, Vesicode researchers along side collaborators from Uppsala University, SciLife Lab and others, describe a novel high throughput, and highly multiplexable proximity-dependent barcoding assay (PBA) to profile surface proteins of exosomes using antibody-DNA conjugates and next-generation sequencing.
